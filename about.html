Sam Herle is a multimedia artist and XR developer in training currently studying in the AEC Virtual & Augmented Reality Specialist program at Champlain College. She also has a BFA in Illustration from Emily Carr University.

Since 2020 she has been commissioned to create multiple virtual art exhibitions for various galleries and organizations (see "digital galleries etc" page), which have been viewed online, projected as interactive installations at various venues throughout Canada, and as visuals for an electronic music event in NYC.
